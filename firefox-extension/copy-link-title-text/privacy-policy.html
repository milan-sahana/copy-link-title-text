Privacy Policy for Copy Link Title Last Updated: 10-Jan-2025 This extension, Copy Link Title, does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data. Permissions used by this extension are solely for its intended functionality: - "clipboardWrite" is used to copy data to the clipboard upon user request. - "contextMenus" is used to provide functionality through the right-click menu. - "host_permissions" and "scripting" are used to interact with the content of the webpage to extract the title attribute of links. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties. If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at contact@milansahana.com.
